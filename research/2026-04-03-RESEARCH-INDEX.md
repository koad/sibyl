- **TAM:** $10–15B by 2030 (unclaimed peer-ring segment)

### Audience
- **Indie developers:** 100K+ on Indie Hackers; isolation is #1 pain
- **Healthcare:** 2,000+ independent provider networks; $300K+ compliance spend
- **Fintech:** 500+ funded agentic AI companies; 224 at Series A+

### Competition
- **LangChain:** Frameworks (no infrastructure)
- **Kaji:** Enterprise on-prem ($500K–$1M solo)
- **SingularityNET:** Crypto-native (regulatory uncertainty)
- **Hugging Face:** Model hub (not agentic)
- **koad:io:** Peer-ring pools (unclaimed segment)

### Sponsors (5 Target Archetypes)
- Healthcare (cost pooling, HIPAA)
- Fintech (PCI-DSS, regulatory clarity)
- Infrastructure (Minio, Equinix, CNCF)
- Government (FedRAMP alternative, sovereignty)
- Developers (Indie Hackers, OSS leaders)

### Timeline
- **Weeks 1–8:** MVP + first 3 sponsors
- **Weeks 8–14:** Launch + all 5 sponsors
- **Success:** 5 sponsors + 10–20 indie developers in first ring
- **Risk window:** Delay beyond 16 weeks = competitor entry risk
