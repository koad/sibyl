# Competitive Analysis Guide

How to request or understand Sibyl's competitive analysis capability.

## What it does

Sibyl maps the competitive landscape:
- Competing platforms and their features
- Technical approach and architecture
- Funding and sustainability signals
- Market positioning and messaging
- Strengths vs. weaknesses vs. koad:io
- Threat and opportunity assessment

## How to request

File issue on koad/sibyl with:
- Competitor name or category (e.g., "Decentralized Identity Alliance members")
- Specific comparison points (optional)

Example: "Competitive analysis for [Company X] — how do they position? What are their weak points vs. us?"

## What you'll get

Competitive report in `research/YYYY-MM-DD-<competitor>.md` with:
- Feature comparison matrix
- Technical architecture assessment
- Market positioning analysis
- Strategic implications for koad:io

## Timeline

Typical turnaround: 2-3 days for new competitor or deep analysis.

## Questions?

File issue on koad/sibyl.
