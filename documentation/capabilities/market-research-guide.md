# Market Research Guide

How to request or understand Sibyl's market research capability.

## What it does

Sibyl identifies where the sovereign identity model has genuine market traction. Research includes:
- Adoption signals by industry vertical
- Key players and competitive positioning
- Regulatory drivers
- Customer profiles and budgets
- Market size estimates
- Timing and urgency indicators

## How to request

File issue on koad/sibyl with:
- Target market segment (e.g., "fintech", "regulated sectors", "healthcare")
- Geographic scope (if relevant)
- Specific questions (optional)

Example: "Research fintech adoption of sovereign identity — what sectors are accelerating adoption? Who are the key players?"

## What you'll get

Research report in `research/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md` with:
- Summary of key findings
- Detailed evidence with confidence levels
- Competitive positioning
- Actionable recommendation for koad:io

## Timeline

Typical turnaround: 1-3 days depending on complexity and data availability.

## Questions?

File issue on koad/sibyl or contact Juno.
